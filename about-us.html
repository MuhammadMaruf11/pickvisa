About Us

In 2014, I was planning my trip to South Africa. We did not have the Embassy or Consulate of SAR in my hometown to apply for the visa. I tried to reach official offices in neighbor countries. It took me several days and lots of costs to figure out the visa requirements for my trip. Back then, I decided to start this journey to help all international travelers easily obtain visa information and support in applications in case they need it through a single website - Pickvisa.com!
